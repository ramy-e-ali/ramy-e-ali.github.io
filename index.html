I am a postdoctoral researcher at Ming Hsieh Department of Electrical Engineering, University of Southern California working with Prof. Salman Avestimehr. I currently work on distributed computing, secure and private machine learning. Beside that, my research interests also include distributed systems, information theory and wireless communications.

I received the Ph.D. degree in electrical engineering from Penn State University, University Park, PA, USA, in 2020. Before that, I received the M.Sc. degree in wireless communications from Wireless Intelligent Networks Center (WINC), Nile University, Giza, Egypt, in 2014 and the B.Sc. degree in electrical engineering from Alexandria University, Alexandria, Egypt, in 2011.
