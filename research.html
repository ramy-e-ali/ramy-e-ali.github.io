Research Interests


PhD Dissertation: Harnessing Data Correlation and Network Information in Distributed Key-Value Stores

My PhD disseration focused on the fundamental limits of strongly consistent key-value stores, developed code constructions based on erasure coding the minimize the storage costs of such systems and studied the latency-consistency trade-off of eventually consistent key-value stores.
