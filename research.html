Energy Harvesters

With the increasing demand for "green" solutions in technology, green communications has also become a rapidly developing field. This field covers a wide range of areas such as energy efficiency and renewable sources in communications.

My current research focuses on optimization and theoretical limits of wireless communication nodes that harvest energy from the surrounding environment. So far I have considered optimization problems such as throughput maximization and transmission completion time minimization in energy harvester nodes under battery restrictions in static and fading channels, for single link and interference settings.
